Stay Connected with TexPREP

TexPREP is more than a summer program—it’s a community that continues to grow through the achievements, experiences, and leadership of its alumni. Whether you participated years ago or recently completed the program, you remain an important part of the TexPREP story.

We invite TexPREP alumni to stay connected, share their journeys, and help inspire the next generation of students interested in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. Your experiences, insights, and accomplishments make a difference—and we’d love to hear from you.

Use the form below to reconnect with TexPREP, update your contact information, share career or academic milestones, volunteer to participate as speaker in career awareness sessions, or simply say hello. Together, we can strengthen the TexPREP network and continue building pathways to success.

Once TexPREP, always TexPREP.
We look forward to hearing from you.
